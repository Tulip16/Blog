Tulip Pandey

Hope

I know no bounds to joy exist,
When the sun shines through the hazy mist.
When a bright smile peeps
Through an old jammed door;
When worries sink
And wishes soar.
When every stair disappears,
Heaven sends the magic rope,
In all dreamy eyes you will see the glint,
You will see the hint of undying hope.
