Tulip Pandey

Realization

When through the sweet confines of a haven, you see,
The world looks so full of meaning.
Merry faces ;Hearts  full of glee ;
Accusing them would seem demeaning.

It doesn't  seem like a believable truth,
For of course you step on the Roses first .
What awaits you is the lack of ruth ,
Crush the thorns next or you get crushed .

Crush them and you feel the pain ,
So you're ready to get crushed instead ;
But isn't it all the same ?
For you step on them and on your soul they tread .

And then there is that sigh of relief ,
For you look around and see lovely smiles .
In a utopia ,they strengthen your belief ,
With a promise of lasting a thousand miles.

You crave to give in to temptation,
And of course you can't resist .
For friendship you build the foundation 
But the pane of trust is now covered with mist .

And no plain mist it is,
For it so terribly hurts your eyes ;
Tears clearing the mist of fake bliss,
Finally making you realize .
