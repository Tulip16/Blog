About

This page is an effort by me to express my opinions
by weaving them into poems or compiling them
into articles.Myriad Musings, reflective of it's name,
is a collection of varied pieces, all of which rise
from the common desire to pour out my feelings onto
an empty canvas in order to adorn it with the jewels of words.
I hope to be able to provide the readers with
quality material from all walks of life. An avid reader
passionate about the rhythms of the soul expressed through
music and dance, I am currently pursuing a degree
in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Bombay.
